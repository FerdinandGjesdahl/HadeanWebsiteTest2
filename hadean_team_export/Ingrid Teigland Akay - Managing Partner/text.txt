Ingrid is a medical doctor and experienced investment professional with background in healthcare, consumer goods and technology. During her investment career, Ingrid has invested into and supported start-up companies globally in different phases of development, from R&D to commercial stage.

As a medical doctor, she gained broad clinical experience working in diverse fields such as general medicine, surgery and psychiatry, with exposure to both the public and private sector in several European countries in Scandinavia, Germany and the UK.

Ingrid holds a medical degree from Medizinische Hochschule Hannover, Germany, and an MBA with Finance Concentration from London Business School.
