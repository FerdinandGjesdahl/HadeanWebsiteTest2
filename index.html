Our Story

A European partner for science-driven innovation

Hadean Ventures is a European venture capital fund manager with over EUR 230 million in assets under management. With a strong foundation and track record in life sciences, the firm invests across sectors and stages, backing solutions that address complex, real-world challenges and unmet needs.

The firm's purpose is to generate strong returns while making a meaningful impact by investing in the future.
